About us

After 10 years as an independent publisher, Mudra became a part of the departmental knowledge platform for Kerala Registration officials, Lawyers, Document Writers, and the Public who are keenly interested to know the prevailing Act, Rules, procedures, and various services being rendered from Registration offices.

                    Mudra, one of its kind with a portfolio of 22 books, which it published such as “Registration Sahayi” is the leading institution to meet the needs of people who are professionally and academically interested in Registration related subjects. Mudra is releasing “Limited Editions” with limited copies. Various eminent personalities are associated with Mudra, and it’s a self-publishing and marketing platform that unleashes the creative genius inside its all readers.

Vision

Builds new and innovative forms of partnership with firms, encouraging them to act as co-creators of robust civil society and amplify success.

Mission

  • Provide new experiences for our customers with creative products and service excellence
  • Make positive contributions to the sustainable development of our society.
  • Enhance stakeholder value by leveraging the Alliance.

Values

Embrace the diversity of people with help of technology and create a solution for humanity.

About Founder

Mudra was founded by Mr. Mohammed Najeeb, the Retd Sub Registrar in 2008 and includes a team of creative and academically conversant veterans of the Registration Department, who share a passion for imparting knowledge voluntarily.
